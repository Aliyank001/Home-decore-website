Our Story

Crafting Beautiful Spaces Since 2011

At Luxe Home Décor, we believe that every space tells a story. Founded with a passion for timeless design and exceptional craftsmanship, we have been transforming houses into homes for over 15 years.

Our journey began with a simple vision: to make luxury home décor accessible to those who appreciate beauty, quality, and artistry. Today, we are proud to be a trusted name in interior design, serving clients across the nation with our curated collections and personalized services.

Every piece in our collection is carefully selected to embody elegance, functionality, and enduring style. We work with renowned designers and artisans worldwide to bring you furniture, art, and accessories that elevate your living experience.

Alexandra Sterling

Founder & Creative Director

Our Mission

To inspire and empower people to create beautiful, personalized living spaces that reflect their unique style and enhance their quality of life through exceptional design and unparalleled customer service.

Our Vision

To be the world's leading destination for luxury home décor, recognized for our commitment to sustainability, innovation in design, and our ability to transform ordinary spaces into extraordinary experiences.
